Rural Education Enhancement System

About

The Rural Education Enhancement System is a web-based platform that provides rural students with access to study materials, mentorship, and other resources to help them succeed in school.

The system also offers information about grants, loans, and incentives to help students and schools bridge the digital divide.

Features

  • Access to study materials
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Skill monitoring
  • Digital divide bridging
  • Grants, loans, and incentives information
  • Connectivity for financially disadvantaged patrons
  • Employment opportunities
  • Research and development
  • Access to material resources
